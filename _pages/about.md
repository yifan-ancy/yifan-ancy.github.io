Hi, I am Yifan Jiang. I am currently an undergraduate student at the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Nanjing University (NJU). I am fortunate to conduct research under the mentorship of [Prof. Beibei Wang](https://wangningbei.github.io/), [Prof. Jieqi Shi](https://jayceeshi.github.io/), and [Prof. Wenbin Li](www.liwenbin.cn), while collaborating closely with Yunkai Dang. These inspiring experiences have not only instilled in me a rigorous scientific methodology but also fueled my persistent passion for exploring the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence.

My previous research experience spans multimodal large model reasoning, trustworthy AI, and embodied AI. Currently, my research interests are focused on two primary directions:

**Neuron Reasoning Attribution**: Dedicated to unveiling the internal decision-making mechanisms of large-scale pre-trained models and exploring how individual neurons synergize to process complex logic.

**Embodied AI**: Focusing on developing agents that can perceive their environments and execute complex tasks, aiming to enhance the operational robustness of robots in the dynamic physical world.
